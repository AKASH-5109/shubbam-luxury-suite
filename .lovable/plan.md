

# Shubbam — Luxury Boutique Website

A premium, single-page women's fashion boutique website with warm earthy tones, elegant typography, and smooth animations.

## Brand & Design
- **Color palette**: Terracotta (#8B4513), warm beige (#F5E6D3), cream (#FFF8F0), charcoal (#2D2D2D), gold accents (#C9A96E)
- **Typography**: Serif headings (Playfair Display), clean sans-serif body (Inter)
- **Style**: Minimal, cinematic, large whitespace — inspired by Dior/Sabyasachi aesthetics
- **Logo**: Shubbam brand logo from uploaded image
